Habits
After a couple of weeks of tracking, one widely-recommended habit is offered at a time — never a notification, always one tap to pass, and permanent when you decline. Accepting keeps it as a quiet focus.
Regimen
A regimen is a named daily template — medications, events, and preset meals at their times, with optional weekday rotation and an eating window. It pre-fills today's checklist at the top of the day; nothing is ever auto-logged. Author it as JSON (template below); food entries reference a saved preset's id.

        Fasting
        
        

          

        

        
        
A gap ≥ this many hours between meals (default 16) becomes a candidate on the day it ended — resolve it "Fasted" or "Ate, didn't log" from the timeline. Pending never counts toward anything. 0-kcal drinks and the daily supplement don't break a fast.
